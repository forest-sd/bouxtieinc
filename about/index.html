About Bouxtie Inc

Bouxtie Inc is a leading provider of employee rewards and recognition solutions. We help organizations of all sizes create meaningful recognition programs that drive engagement, boost morale, and improve retention.

Our Mission

We believe that every employee deserves to feel valued. Our platform makes it easy for organizations to recognize and reward their team members in ways that are personal, timely, and impactful. From digital gift cards to customizable reward catalogs, we provide the tools needed to build a culture of appreciation.

What We Offer

Our comprehensive suite of solutions includes digital reward delivery, incentive management platforms, performance-based reward programs, customizable recognition initiatives, and analytics to measure the impact of your recognition efforts. Whether you're a small business or a large enterprise, our flexible platform adapts to your needs.
